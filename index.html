About Me

I'm a PhD student in the Department of Biological Systems Engineering at UNL, advised by Dr. Tami Brown-Brandl. My dissertation research focuses on developing techniques in precision poultry management using deep learning-based computer vision pipelines.

I hold a Master's degree in Crop, Soil and Environmental Science from Virginia Tech, where I worked on robotics applications in precision spraying. I also earned a Graduate Certificate in Data Analytics & Artificial Intelligence.

Prior to graduate school, I completed my Bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering from Tribhuvan University in Nepal and worked as a Freelance Software Developer. You can reach me at mr(dot)aashiz(at)gmail.com
